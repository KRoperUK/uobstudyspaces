Keyboard controls:

  • H to toggle between showing all and only open spaces
  • 1-9 to go forward in time by 1-9 hours
  • R to go back to the current time
  • [ and ] or Space to go back or forward by 1 day
  • ← and → to go back or forward by 1 hour
  • + and - to zoom in and out
